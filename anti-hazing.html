Anti-Hazing Policy

As fraternity members, the tragedies and brutal practices that have occurred because of hazing is at the forefront of our mind. The practice of hazing is cruel and senseless, which is why here at the Lambda Theta Delta fraternity we practice a strict zero tolerance policy for hazing.

Our program was designed to have as little stress as possible with the goal to help our members grow together. Your well-being is our number one priority.

For any questions or concerns, please reach us via our email: ltdfraternity@gmail.com
