Rush

Rush happens in week 1 of the Fall and Winter quarter, with bids being offered at the end of the week. Rush is important for us to get to know you as a person and more importantly, for you to see if we're the right fit. Over the course of the week, rushees have multiple interactions with current members, sister sororities, fellow rushees and get to meet the alumni during alumni night to get some insight into their life after being part of the fraternity. Don't be shy!

You can find out more about the program after accepting the bid in our "Program" page.

Interested? DM our Instagram, linked below.

FIRST SOCIAL

Start your week off right with a night of fun with the brothers of LTD and a guest sister sorority in a joint rush event.

BROS NIGHT

Get to know the brothers of Lambda Theta Delta personally over free food and drinks.

INFO NIGHT

One of the most important night, come learn the history of Lambda Theta Delta and the true meaning of brotherhood.

SECOND SOCIAL

Come spend another night with the brothers of LTD and guest sister sororities. This will be your last chance to meet the bros before Bid Night.

BID NIGHT

End the week of fun at this Invite Only event where we determine if we're the right fit for you and hand out bids.
