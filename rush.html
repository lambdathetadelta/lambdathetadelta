Rush

Rush happens in week 1 of Fall and Winter Quarter, with bids being offered at the end of Rush week. Rush is important for us to get to know each rushee as a person and more importantly, for you to see if we are the right fit.
Over the course of the week, rushees will have multiple opportunities to interaction with current members, sister sororities, fellow rushees and the alumni during alumni night to get some insight into life after being part of the fraternity. Don't be shy!
You can find out more about the program after accepting the bid in our "Program" page.
