Philanthrophy

As an institution of opportunity, the Lambda Theta Delta fraternity is a strong believer in socioeconomic equity. Seeking to tackle systemic injustices, LTD serves underrepresented communities through philanthropy and service.

Issues we're focused on right now

  • UCI4COLA - UCI's very own grassroots mutual aid network

  • Equal Justice Initiative - A proponent of prison reform

  • AAAJ - Asian American civil rights organization

  • UC Student Workers Union - We stand with our graduate workers
